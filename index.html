Intro

I am a highly skilled web developer with a diverse technical expertise that encompasses a broad spectrum of technologies and frameworks. My journey in the world of software development has been marked by a commitment to excellence, an insatiable curiosity for emerging technologies, and an unwavering dedication to building reliable and secure web applications.

Proficiency in an array of technologies is the cornerstone of my skill set. They include but not limited to Java Spring, Kotlin, C# (.NET framework), and the Android framework. I can quickly adapt and learn new languages and libraries. In Android, a milestone was achieved in 2022 when I earned certification as a Google Developer in Android through the Google Africa Developer scholarship program. Throughout this experience, I not only gained invaluable knowledge but also had the privilege of collaborating with talented individuals from across Africa, forming lasting connections and friendships. In the project phase, my team's project, where I was the lead Android Developer, was shortlisted among the last 10 teams for the last round. It was an honour and a humnbling experience.

Beyond the realm of programming, my heart lies in the intricate world of networks and the profound impact of the internet on global communication, effectively shrinking the world into a connected village. As a Cisco Certified Network Associate(CCNA) certified professional, I have delved into the depths of networking, and my passion for this field knows no bounds. My aspiration is not just to consume innovations in networking but to lead the charge in inventing new solutions that shape the future of this discipline.
